Lumen Guard Privacy Policy

Lumen Guard uses the AccessibilityService API to create a system-wide overlay that dims your screen and filters blue light. This is the only purpose of this permission.

Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data, screen content, or personal information. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Ads: We use AppLovin for monetization. They may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads.
