Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy matters to us. Lumen Guard is designed with privacy as a core principle. We minimize data collection and only use third-party advertising services to support the free version of the app. We are committed to transparency about what data is collected and how it's used.

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumen Guard ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using Lumen Guard, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection Overview

Lumen Guard operates on a privacy-respecting model:

  • By Lumen Guard directly: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our app
  • By advertising partners: The free version of our app uses third-party advertising networks which may collect certain data as described below
  • Premium version: Users who upgrade to Premium have NO ads and NO third-party data collection

Information We (Lumen Guard) DO NOT Collect

Lumen Guard itself does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage statistics or analytics data
  • Location data
  • Screen content or screenshots
  • App usage patterns or behavior
  • Browsing history or other app usage
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

Note: While we don't collect this data, our advertising partner (AppLovin MAX) may collect certain information as described in the "Advertising and Third-Party Services" section below.

Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system. This data includes:

  • Dim and warmth level preferences
  • Custom scene configurations
  • Schedule settings
  • App configuration options (shake to toggle, auto-start, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions We Require

Lumen Guard requires the following Android permissions to function:

1. SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (Draw Over Other Apps)

This permission is essential for Lumen Guard to display the dimming overlay on top of other applications. This is the core functionality that allows the app to dim your screen and apply the blue light filter system-wide.

2. FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE

These permissions allow Lumen Guard to run in the background and maintain the screen filter even when you switch to other apps. This ensures continuous eye protection without draining your battery.

3. INTERNET (Free Version Only - For Ads)

Required to display advertisements via AppLovin MAX in the free version. This permission enables the app to download ad content and communicate with advertising servers. Premium users have no ads and no advertising network communication.

Important: Lumen Guard itself does not use these permissions to collect or transmit your personal data. The INTERNET permission is solely used by AppLovin MAX for advertising purposes in the free version.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Third-Party Advertising (Free Version Only)

The free version of Lumen Guard displays advertisements through third-party advertising networks and mediation platforms. These advertising partners may collect and process certain information to provide personalized ads and measure ad performance.

Information Collected by Advertising Networks

When you use the free version of Lumen Guard, our advertising partners and their networks may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, screen size, device language
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (GAID) or other advertising identifiers
  • IP Address: Your device's IP address (used to determine approximate location at country/city level)
  • App Information: App name, version, and package name
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about which ads you viewed, clicked, or interacted with
  • Performance Data: Information about ad loading times and errors

Purpose of Data Collection

Advertising networks use this information to:

  • Deliver relevant advertisements to you
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Improve advertising services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Advertising Partners

Lumen Guard may work with one or more advertising networks and mediation platforms to display ads. These partners may also collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. Advertising partners may include (but are not limited to):

  • Google AdMob
  • AppLovin MAX
  • Meta Audience Network
  • Unity Ads
  • Vungle
  • ironSource
  • AdColony
  • Other advertising networks

The specific advertising partners we use may change over time. For current information about our advertising partners and their privacy practices, please review:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Information
  • AppLovin Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • Other partners' privacy policies (accessible through their respective websites)

Your Advertising Choices

You have control over personalized advertising:

How to Opt-Out of Personalized Ads on Android:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Privacy → Ads (or Google → Ads)
  3. Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  4. You can also "Reset advertising ID" to clear your ad profile

Note: Opting out means you'll still see ads, but they won't be personalized based on your interests.

Additional opt-out options:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance

Remove Ads Entirely - Upgrade to Premium

Want to eliminate all advertising and third-party data collection? Upgrade to Lumen Guard Premium. Premium users enjoy:

  • ✨ Zero ads - No advertisements whatsoever
  • ✨ No third-party tracking - All advertising networks completely removed
  • ✨ Complete privacy - No external data collection
  • ✨ Plus all premium features (unlimited scenes, advanced scheduling, etc.)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We want to be completely transparent about when and how data is shared:

Data Shared by Lumen Guard

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade any personal data. Lumen Guard itself does not have access to or control over the data collected by advertising networks.

Data Shared by Advertising Networks

Advertising networks may share data with:

  • Advertising Partners: Ad networks that bid to show you advertisements
  • Analytics Providers: To measure ad performance and campaign effectiveness
  • Fraud Prevention Services: To detect and prevent invalid ad traffic
  • Service Providers: Companies that help operate the advertising platform

Circumstances for Data Disclosure

Data may be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (users will be notified)
  • With Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Internet Permission

Lumen Guard requires the INTERNET permission to display advertisements in the free version. This permission allows advertising networks to:

  • Download and display ad content
  • Communicate with advertising servers
  • Report ad performance metrics

Premium version note: Even though the permission remains (Android requirement), Premium users have ads completely disabled and no advertising network communication occurs.

In-App Purchases

Lumen Guard offers an optional premium upgrade through Google Play's in-app purchase system. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is processed entirely by Google Play
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Google Play's privacy policy governs the collection and use of payment data
  • We only receive a confirmation that the purchase was successful

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Lumen Guard does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. However, we take additional precautions regarding advertising:

  • We do not knowingly direct ads to children under 13
  • AppLovin MAX is configured to comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information

Parents and guardians: If you believe your child has used Lumen Guard and provided information, please contact us immediately at privacy@beansnbrackets.com

Recommendation: For children under 13, we strongly recommend using the Premium version (no ads, no tracking) with parental supervision.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Lumen Guard's Data: All your app settings are stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • Advertising Data: Data collected by AppLovin MAX is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Third-Party Security: AppLovin and its partners maintain their own security measures. Please review their privacy policies for details

Premium Users: Enjoy the highest level of privacy with no third-party data transmission whatsoever.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the app or via the Google Play Store
  • Provide you with an opportunity to review the changes

Your continued use of Lumen Guard after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

To exercise these rights regarding advertising data, please contact the respective advertising networks through their privacy portals or use the industry opt-out tools linked above.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know: Request details about data collected, used, or shared
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of personal information

  • Right to non-discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To opt-out of targeted advertising (CCPA "sale"), use your device's ad personalization settings.
