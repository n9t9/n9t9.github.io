Lumen Guard

Professional Eye Protection for Android

A powerful screen dimmer and blue light filter designed to protect your eyes during extended screen time. Take control of your display with advanced dimming, customizable warmth, and intelligent scheduling.

Download App Learn More

Powerful Features

Adjustable Screen Dimming

Reduce screen brightness beyond system limits with precise control. Perfect for late-night reading or dark environments.

Blue Light Filter

Apply customizable warmth levels to reduce blue light emission and minimize eye strain during prolonged use.

Custom Scenes

Save your favorite dim and warmth combinations for quick access. Switch between reading, movie, and bedtime modes instantly.

Smart Scheduling

Automate filter activation with time-based schedules. Set your preferred times and let Lumen Guard handle the rest.

Shake to Toggle

Quickly enable or disable the screen filter by shaking your device. Fast and convenient control at your fingertips.

Auto-Start on Boot

Automatically launch Lumen Guard when your device starts. Your eye protection never takes a break.

Lightweight & Fast

Minimal battery impact with optimized performance. Efficient overlay technology ensures smooth operation.

Privacy First

No data collection, no ads, no tracking. Your privacy is our priority. All settings stored locally on your device.
