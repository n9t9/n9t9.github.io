Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and get help with Lumen Guard

Getting Started

How do I install Lumen Guard?

Installing Lumen Guard is simple:

  1. Download Lumen Guard from the Google Play Store
  2. Open the app after installation
  3. Grant the "Draw over other apps" permission when prompted
  4. Start adjusting your screen dimming and warmth levels!
üí° Tip: The overlay permission is essential for Lumen Guard to work. Without it, the app cannot display the dimming filter over other applications.
What permissions does Lumen Guard need and why?

Lumen Guard requires the following permissions:

  • Draw over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) - Required to display the dimming overlay on your screen
  • Foreground Service - Allows the app to keep running in the background to maintain your settings

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's core functionality. We do not collect or transmit any data. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I enable the screen filter?

To enable the screen filter:

  1. Open Lumen Guard
  2. On the Home screen, toggle the power button at the top
  3. Adjust the dim and warmth sliders to your preference
  4. The filter will be applied system-wide

Features & Usage

What are Custom Scenes and how do I use them?

Custom Scenes allow you to save your favorite dim and warmth combinations for quick access. For example, you can create:

  • Reading Mode - Medium dim, high warmth
  • Movie Mode - Low dim, low warmth
  • Bedtime Mode - High dim, maximum warmth

To create a scene:

  1. Go to the Scenes tab
  2. Tap "Create New Scene" (Premium feature)
  3. Adjust dim and warmth levels
  4. Give your scene a name and save
üí° Tip: Custom Scenes are a Premium feature. Free users have access to default scenes.
How does Smart Scheduling work?

Smart Scheduling automatically enables the screen filter at specific times. Perfect for evening eye protection!

To set up a schedule:

  1. Go to the Schedule tab
  2. Toggle "Enable Schedule"
  3. Set your start time (e.g., 8:00 PM)
  4. Set your end time (e.g., 7:00 AM)
  5. Lumen Guard will automatically activate during these hours
What is "Shake to Toggle"?

"Shake to Toggle" is a quick gesture control that lets you enable or disable the screen filter by shaking your device.

To enable it:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Toggle "Shake to Toggle" on
  3. Give your phone a firm shake to toggle the filter on/off
üí° Tip: This is great when you need to quickly disable the filter to view colors accurately, then shake again to re-enable it.
What's the difference between Dim and Warmth?
  • Dim Level - Controls the overall darkness of the screen overlay. Higher values make your screen darker, reducing brightness beyond system limits.
  • Warmth Level - Applies a warm (orange/amber) tint to reduce blue light emission. Higher values provide more blue light filtering.

For best eye protection, especially before bedtime, use both a moderate dim level and high warmth level.

Troubleshooting

The filter isn't working. What should I do?

If the filter isn't displaying, try these steps:

  1. Check Permissions - Go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Lumen Guard ‚Üí Permissions and ensure "Display over other apps" is enabled
  2. Restart the App - Close and reopen Lumen Guard
  3. Check if Enabled - Make sure the power toggle on the Home screen is ON
  4. Check Battery Settings - Some devices aggressively kill background apps. Add Lumen Guard to your battery optimization exceptions
The filter disappears when I switch apps

This usually happens due to battery optimization settings:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Battery or Battery Optimization
  3. Find Lumen Guard and set it to "Don't optimize" or "Unrestricted"
  4. Enable "Auto-Start on Boot" in Lumen Guard settings
‚ö†Ô∏è Note: Different manufacturers have different battery optimization menus. Search for "battery optimization" in your device settings.
How do I exclude certain apps from the filter?

Currently, Lumen Guard applies the filter system-wide. If you need to temporarily disable it for specific tasks:

  • Use the quick toggle on the Home screen
  • Enable "Shake to Toggle" for quick gesture control
  • Pull down your notification shade and tap the Lumen Guard notification to quickly toggle
üí° Future Feature: Per-app filtering is planned for a future update!
The app is affecting my battery life

Lumen Guard is designed to be lightweight and battery-efficient. However, if you notice increased battery drain:

  • Make sure you're running the latest version from Google Play
  • Reduce the frequency of shake detection if you don't use that feature
  • The overlay itself uses minimal resources (less than 1% typically)

If battery drain persists, please contact support with your device model and Android version.

How do I uninstall Lumen Guard?

To uninstall Lumen Guard:

  1. First, disable the screen filter within the app
  2. Go to your device Settings ‚Üí Apps
  3. Find Lumen Guard and tap "Uninstall"

All your settings and data will be automatically removed when you uninstall.
